Alberto de la Cruz

I am a product designer focusing on consumer experiences.

I’m a designer, web developer, educator, and mentor with almost a decade of experience in the tech space. I'm the founder of Token Interactive, a digital design agency, and am also a product design mentor at Designlab. Previously, I was a course lead at the Parsons School of Design and worked as Stayawhile's lead product designer.

To me, design is visual engineering. I’m great at hitting tight deadlines by figuring out simple solutions to complex problems, cutting the right corners in the process. As someone with an extensive background in tech, design, and everything in between I excel at wearing many hats and making the impossible possible.
